Instructions

1. Open DevTools (F12)

2. Go to the "DevTools Helper" panel

3. Click "Find Colors" in the "Colors" section

4. You should see both RGB and RGBA colors listed

5. RGBA colors should show with transparency indicators and 8-digit hex codes
